Shipping & Delivery Information

Understanding our commitment to timely and safe delivery of your organic produce.

Delivery Information

Delivery Areas

We proudly deliver across all major cities in Pakistan. For remote areas, please contact our customer service to confirm eligibility before placing your order.

  • Karachi
  • Lahore
  • Islamabad
  • Rawalpindi
  • Faisalabad
  • Multan
  • And more...

Shipping Times

Our standard delivery times are as follows:

  • Major Cities: 2-3 business days
  • Other Cities: 4-7 business days
  • Orders placed after 3 PM will be processed on the next business day.
  • Weekend orders are processed on Monday.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your order total and delivery location. We offer free shipping for orders above a certain value.

  • Standard Shipping: PKR 200
  • Free Shipping: For orders above PKR 5000

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can use this number to track your delivery status on our partner courier's website.

For any tracking issues, please contact our support team.

Important Notes

  • Ensure accurate delivery address and contact number to avoid delays.
  • We are not responsible for delays caused by unforeseen circumstances (e.g., natural disasters, political unrest).
  • For any changes to your order or delivery address, please contact us immediately.
  • All organic products are carefully packaged to maintain freshness during transit.
  • Delivery times may vary during peak seasons and holidays.
